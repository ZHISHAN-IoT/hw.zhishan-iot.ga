What is HML?

HML is an open-source project that provides various lite and easy-use firmware libraries & BSPs for some widely used MCUs, such as the 8051 series and the STC89 series, or their development boards (refer to support-list for details). The slogan of HML, “Provide a layer between hardware and originality”, reveals that the main purpose of HML is to help developers to develop these devices more easily and quickly.

The source files of HML are all written in C language. However, different components may rely on different development tools. For example, the HML_FwLib_8051 is developed for SDCC, while others might be developed for Keil, or avr-gcc etc. Please refer to the detail page of each component for specifics.

You can download or make contributions to HML projects through GitHub. Welcome the advices from you, thanks!

What MCU or board is being supported by HML now?

The table is support list of HML:

  • MCUcorresponding component
  • updIntel MCS-51 based microprocessors (8031, 8032, 8051, 8052, etc.)
  • updSTC micro STC10 series (STC10F04, IAP10F04, etc.)
  • planSTC micro STC11 series (STC11F02E, STC11F04E, etc.)
  • updSTC micro STC89 series (STC89C52RC, STC89C54RD, etc.)
  • updSTC micro STC90-RC/RD+ series (STC90C52RC, STC90C54RD+, etc.)
  • planSTC micro STC90-AD series (STC90C58AD, STC90LE516AD, etc.)
  • BOARD corresponding component
  • soonTS-51 V2.0
  • planZS5110

*Note: Italic item means that it will be online soon or offline temporarily.
